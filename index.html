My Igloo Poster by Ben Lamb

My biggest takeaways from the last two days: Igloo is extremely customer focused (e.g. approach to customer service, security, Ofgem) The people and processes are very straightforward, honest, and respectful
One thing I've discovered about Igloo Igloo is up there with other leading energy suppliers, providing competitive products and services including one simple variable tariff, air source heat pumps, smart thermostats, and mobile apps used to manage your energy and smart EV charging.
Bringing the Igloo values into being a Full Stack Engineer
  • Customer First - build websites and apps to be usable, dependable, accessible, and delightful for the customer.
  • Straightforward - use the best practices to do the right thing, be honest, and get the job done.
  • Passionate - take responsibility for my own development by keeping up-to-date with modern tech.
  • Focused - embrace feedback, use an agile methodology, and set clear development objectives that are data led.
The one thing I will do in the next 30 days... ...is get caught up and comfortable with the tech and languages that I will be using.
